# Quick Task 260402-l91: Replace RichTextEditor with QWebEngineView Summary

**One-liner:** QTextEdit-based RichTextEditor replaced by offline Quill 1.3.7 editor via QWebEngineView with processEvents-polling sync HTML extraction and html/head/body wrapper stripping.

---

## What Was Done

Replaced the custom QTextEdit-based `RichTextEditor` in the A250 form's `project_description` and `detailed_scope` fields with a Quill-powered `WebRichTextEditor` embedded in a `QWebEngineView`. Quill runs entirely offline from local assets — no CDN requests. The `html_to_richtext` parser was updated to strip html/head/body wrappers that Quill (and QTextEdit) both produce.
